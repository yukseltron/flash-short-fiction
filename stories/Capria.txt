PRELUDE:
King Luptin To'mis was the youngest son of the famed and esteemed Alverian King and Conqueror, Albrecht Roos To'mis. Albretch had restored the To'mis dynasty, successfully ensured the security of Alverian mainlands during the rise of the Tryvan empire under Emperor Tenus, following Mosoro the Visionary's ideals. Albrecht was a tough, proven and powerful lord of alveria. His 52-year reign would be among the longest and a lone straight of stability in what would prove to be troubling times for the Kingdom of the Eastern Sun. However, Albrecht's greatest failure's would surely return to haunt the kingdom of Alveria. Namely, was his succession. Albrecht had intended his oldest of 2 sons, Prince Tomyn To'mis to be heir. Prince Tomyn was prime for the position too. A proven warrior and charming leader, Tomyn gave hope for a continued and stable future. However, this was all dashed after Tomyn's ill timed death at the age of 23 during the Battle of Sarkas, during the tragic war for Rivalden. Tyrvish armies were eager to secure Rivalden, long contested ally of Alveria. Tomyn's death not only led to the fall of Rivalden, but threw succession for Albrecht into disarray, for he had but one lame son to bestow his title to. Albrecht would have to have his hopes lie on his second son Mertil, as he himself would die just 2 years after Tomyn.

Prince Mertil To'mis was thrusted into kingship under tragic circumstances. With his father and brother both having died in the last 2 years during a critical war against the rising Tryvan Empire, the Alverian Kingdom was thrown into uncertainty. Mertil would prove to be an adequate leader, however his numerous failed counter campaigns against Tyrve would ultimately result in the secession of many important lands for Alveria, namely lands his father had worked so long and hard to protect previously. Though Mertil would reign for 23 years, he became a larger figurehead at year 15 after the disastrous effort to reclaim Rivalden. He would die of illness, however not before producing 3 young men all eager to restore the great kingdom Albrecht had built not so long before. The young champions of Alveria were heralded as the new trio of hope, similar to the great 3 that liberated Alveria in the old era from the first Tyrvan Empire. 

Initially, this seemed to be the case. Prince Saval, equippped with his sabre of death and shadows, Oscura, successfully lead counter invasion campaigns against a Tyrve empire transitioning and handling its new expanses. Saval was prime to be the next king, and his liberation of Rivalden was a huge factor. However, Alverian Law dictated that without a stated heir, the council was bestowed the choice of who would be King next. Prince Amdal, also proved to be a capable commander and general. With his sword of sun and flames, Firenze, Amdal successfully defeated invading Almaran forces at the siege of Valer, capital of Alveria.

Two of the 3 brother would prove contentious to each other. Neither Saval or Amdal got along, as both were keenly aware of the other's conflicting ambitions. With the Tyrvish and Almaran threats sated for the time being, the brothers turned to each other. After discovering the council was to choose Amdal as the new King, Saval obliterated the council. ALthough public opinion was on Saval's side, Amdal had gained the council's favour with his spoils of war gained from defeating and counter invading Almaran ports. With Amdal discovering this, and Rava's purchase granting Amdal a massive slave army, the stage was set for a civil war. The King of Death was to fight the King of Slaves.
