
==\--in--spite--of--the--blade---------

The blade slowly creases out of its sheath.
The long yet swift reveal slices through the air.
A glint of death streaks across, hitting her eyes squarely.

It catches her off guard, but only for a moment. What she felt was her body contemplating
a possible fight or flight. She could feel the adrenaline gearing her body for tension and
excitement. 

She feels the weight shift to her toes as they dig further into the ground. She must be 
keen in this scenario, and try to stay alert of any attack from the blade. Though difficult it will be to avoid the sweeps and jabs, as long as she primes herself she has
a chance.
